About Us

Our founding ideal is thus: "Gather, tabletop, drinks!" Since then we've been providing table space, beverages, mediocre food, and a safe environment for everyone to enjoy each others company! Our founders R.U. Sirius and Nu F.N. Weigh realized their local game stores were missing something critical and decided it was their responsibility to fix this. Now you can safely eat & drink your sorrows away while your friends continue to roll dice like they're blessed by the gods.

Tables

Of course, the biggest selling point is the amount of space you have to slam any and all board games that make you want to throw your entire collection into a fire, plus a little more to hold the refreshments! We have over 20 tables available and each of are available for purchase at a discounted rate of a new table! If you'd like to order a new table, inquire with the owner or fill out a form on this website. Each table is custom made to order!


Mediocre Food
