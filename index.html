About Us

Our founding ideal is thus: "Gather, tabletop, drinks!" Since then we've been providing table space, beverages, mediocre food, and a safe environment for everyone to enjoy each others company! Our founders R.U. Sirius and Nu F.N. Weigh realized their local game stores were missing something critical and decided it was their responsibility to fix this. Now you can safely eat & drink your sorrows away while your friends continue to roll dice like they're blessed by the gods.
